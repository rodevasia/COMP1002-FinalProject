About Us

Our Story

"At Print Hub, we're on a mission to bring back the charm of vintage writing. Nestled within the keystrokes of every old typewriter lies a story waiting to be told, a piece of history waiting to be rediscovered. From classic Olivettis to iconic Royals, each typewriter we curate holds its own unique tale, echoing the nostalgia of bygone eras. Our passion for preserving the art of tangible writing drives us to meticulously restore and showcase these timeless machines, offering writers, collectors, and enthusiasts alike the opportunity to embark on their own journey through time with each click-clack of the keys. Welcome to Print Hub, where every typewriter is a gateway to a world of imagination and inspiration."

Our Passion

"At Print Hub, we're not just about typewriters. We're also passionate about preserving the tools and accessories that make the writing experience truly unique. Our collection includes vintage ink bottles, pens, and paper, each carrying its own piece of history and nostalgia. We believe in celebrating the art of writing in all its forms, from the clickety-clack of typewriter keys to the smooth flow of ink on paper. Join us on our journey to rediscover the joy of tangible writing at Print Hub."

Our Vision

"Print Hub envisions a world where the beauty of vintage writing is celebrated and cherished by all. We strive to create a community of writers, collectors, and enthusiasts who share our passion for preserving the art of tangible writing. Through our curated collection of vintage typewriters, accessories, and resources, we aim to inspire creativity, evoke nostalgia, and foster a deeper appreciation for the written word. Join us in our mission to keep the spirit of vintage writing alive at Print Hub."
